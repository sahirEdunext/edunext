Science Olympiad For Class

Learn O Test World Science Olympiad is uniquely designed for the students of Class with an approach to provide them an international competition exposure. For the LoT World Olympiad, the student is required to prepare for the olympiad. For the preparation of the olympiad, LoT is providing study materials, which are designed under the guidance of the teachers and professors associated with well-reputed schools and colleges around the nation. Students can access the study materials from the official website of LoT.

How to enroll for the LoT World Olympiad?

To enroll in LOT World Olympiad follow the four easy steps

  • Step 1)  Click on register now

  • Step 2)  Fill in the details required

  • Step 3)  Pay the amount

  • Step 4)  Click on submit

Note:

  • After submitting all the details the student will receive a unique id with a password on the registered mail id or phone number.

  • By using that id and password the student will be able to login during the exams and practice sessions.

  • Kindly provide a valid mail id and phone number as further details and deals will be shared there only.
