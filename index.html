Process to Enroll for Olympiad

Participating in LoT World Olympiads is just like 1-2-3-4. Follow the steps mentioned below right from registration to rewards.

Step 1

Furnish your basic details.

Step 2

Get prepared for LoT World Olympiads by accessing content, worksheets, and practice tests.

Step 3

Appear for online Olympiads as per the schedule and do your best following examination ethics.

Step 4

You are the winner, see yourself on the top of the Leaderboard. Exciting prizes waiting for you.
